Privacy Policy – VoiceCal

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VoiceCal (available at https://voicecal-two.vercel.app) handles information when you use the application.

1. Information We Access

VoiceCal uses Google OAuth 2.0 to request access to your Google Calendar.

VoiceCal requests permission only to:

  • Create calendar events
  • Modify calendar events
  • View calendar events necessary to perform requested actions

VoiceCal does not access Gmail, Google Drive, Google Contacts, or any other Google services. VoiceCal only accesses Google Calendar data for the authenticated user who grants permission.

2. How We Use Information

Google Calendar data is used solely to create, modify, or manage calendar events based on your voice commands.

VoiceCal does not use Google user data for advertising, marketing, analytics, profiling, or any secondary purposes.

3. Data Storage

VoiceCal does not permanently store Google Calendar data on its servers. Data is processed only as necessary to complete the requested action. Calendar operations occur directly between your browser and Google’s secure APIs.

4. Data Sharing

VoiceCal does not sell, rent, or share Google user data with third parties.

5. Revoking Access

You may revoke VoiceCal’s access to your Google account at any time by visiting:

https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

6. Compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy

VoiceCal’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
