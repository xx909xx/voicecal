Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VoiceCal is a voice-powered tool that creates events in your Google Calendar.

Information We Access

VoiceCal requests permission to create and manage events in your Google Calendar. It does not access or read existing calendar events unless necessary to create new ones.

Data Storage

VoiceCal does not store your personal data on any server. All calendar actions are performed directly through Google's secure APIs.

Third-Party Services

VoiceCal uses Google OAuth and Google Calendar API to create events on your behalf.

Data Sharing

VoiceCal does not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.
